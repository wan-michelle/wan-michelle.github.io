MEZANGELLE

>-Mezangelle is a mixture of programming language and informal speech invented by Mez Breeze in 1993 to rearrange and dissect English. It fuses English, poetic conventions, programming code, online communiqué, contemporary social commentary. Her intentions is to explore the relationship between machine and human languages, e.g.

>>--"::Art.hro][botic][scopic N.][in][ten][dos][tions::"

>>>--Mezangelle poetry also has conventions of programming language, like [b:g:in]

>>--Her work tries to question a culture of exclusion, "right" vs. "wrong", so there are multiple ways to interpret her work.

>-"arthroscopic" / "art robotic"/ "Arthrobotic" / "horoscopic" and “Nintendos" / "intentions" / "DOS"
